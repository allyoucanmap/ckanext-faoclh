Climate and Land Hub

The aim of this hub is to provide comprehensive resources to mobilize ambition and actions in the agriculture and land sectors in relation to climate change. This hub intends to respond to countries needs of stakeholder engagement and capacity development, facilitating access to effective tools, guidance, policy advice and learning material in order to support decision makers and practitioners to deliver on their climate and sustainable development goals.

You can navigate through our resources by looking at climate change topics or at your specific activity needs, as well as at a particular type of resources.
